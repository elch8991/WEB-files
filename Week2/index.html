The History of Pizza

When people think of pizza they typically think of Italy, but flatbreads

were consumed starting in ancient times by Greeks, Romans, and Egyptians.

The pizza we recognize in modern times orginated in the city of Naples.

Naples was a waterfront city with a large population of impoverished citizens.

They needed inexpensive food that could be eaten quickly on the street.

While most people rejected the consumption of the flatbreads, in 1889 King Umberto I and Queen Margherita

visited Naples and on their visit dined on what is the modern day margarita pizza.

their approval of the dish led to the spread of pizza popularity and the spark

that has led to the modern staple of pizza that we know today.
